Frequently asked questions

We accept MP3 and WAV files for audio ads. The file must be 30 seconds long for standard radio spots or 60 seconds for extended spots. Minimum bitrate of 192kbps is required. Stereo audio is preferred.
AdCreator uses generative AI to write and produce your radio ad in real time. Simply enter a brief describing your business, your offer, and your audience. The AI will generate a script and produce a voiceover within minutes. You can listen back, edit the script, and regenerate as many times as you like before approving.
After submitting your brief, our creative team will be in touch within 2 working days to discuss your campaign. The full production process typically takes 14–21 days, which is why we recommend booking at least 3 weeks before your campaign start date.
